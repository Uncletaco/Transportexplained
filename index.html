Prep checklist

Ready your vehicle

  • Wash and document condition (photos from all angles).
  • Remove toll tags and personal items; keep weight minimal.
  • Fuel at ~¼ tank for safety and loading.
  • Note any quirks (low clearance, dead battery, alarms).
  • Provide a backup contact in case plans change day‑of.
Timing

How long does it take?

Transit varies by distance, route, and weather. As a planning guideline, many routes average about two days per 500 miles. We’ll give you a live ETA once your carrier is assigned.

FAQ

Common questions

Can I put belongings in the car?

We recommend keeping items under the window line and within 100 lbs total. Please avoid valuables and anything loose that could shift during transit.

Do I need to be there at pickup?

A designated adult must be present for inspection and handoff. If you can’t be there, we can coordinate with an authorized representative.

What if my car is inoperable?

We ship in‑op vehicles too. Let us know ahead of time so we arrange a winch‑equipped carrier and allow for extra loading time.

Is my vehicle insured in transit?

Yes—standard cargo coverage is included by the assigned carrier. We can review coverage limits for your specific shipment and provide options if you’d like higher limits.
